Physics is Fun!

Sound is a type of energy that we hear, like music or a bird's chirp. It travels as waves through air, water, or other materials, reaching our ears.Sound is made when things vibrate, like when you clap your hands or pluck a guitar string.

Atoms are tiny building blocks that make up everything around us, like toys, water, and even our bodies. Each atom has a center called the nucleus, with electrons moving around it. Atoms join together to form molecules, creating all kinds of materials and substances.

Light is a type of energy that helps us see things. It comes from sources like the sun, lamps, and flashlights, traveling in straight lines. Light is what makes everything visible around us, like the way sunlight brightens the day..

Chemistry is Amazing!

Chemistry helps us understand the makeup of everything around us. From the air we breathe to the food we eat, it's all about molecules!

Reactions like fizzing soda or rusting iron show us how substances change and create new things.

The periodic table organizes elements like oxygen and gold, helping us understand their properties and uses.
