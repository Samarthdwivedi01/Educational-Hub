Facts to Know!

Did you know? Light takes only 8 minutes and 20 seconds to travel from the Sun to Earth, covering 149.6 million kilometers!

Chemistry helps us understand the makeup of everything around us. From the air we breathe to the food we eat, it's all about molecules!

There are more bacteria in your body than human cells. In fact, bacteria make up about 3% of your total body mass!

Gravity is not the same everywhere on Earth. You weigh slightly less at the equator than at the poles because of Earth's shape.

Reactions like fizzing soda or rusting iron show how substances change and form new compounds through chemical processes.

The human brain contains around 86 billion neurons and consumes approximately 20% of the body's total energy.
